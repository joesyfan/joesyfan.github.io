Hey! I am Jun, a product designer at Carta. Currently based in Seattle, WA.

design

My fondness for art and design stemmed from experimenting with different types of painting and musical instruments as a kid. With more than 8 years of fine art training, I'm familiar with visual language, as well as the emotions and cultures it implies.

Later gained a master degree in digital media from University of Washington, I have a deep understanding towards technology and design trade-offs. In the past four years, I strive to solve problems for people, and to visualize solutions for all stages of design.

View résumé or selected projects.

...or not design

I explore all sorts of possibilities of myself through:

visual arts -
Photography and painting free me from language and words, and help me capture subtle moments and feelings. I am also a die-hard cinephile.

music and noise -
I am an amateur pianist, a former guitarist in a rock band, and a flute player in an orchestra, and I am learning modular synth currently. Having been playing music for most of my life (over 20 years), I figured that I am a better designer than musician. I am an all-time eager listener though - my faves are: 90s indie, experimental, shoegaze, techno, free jazz, classical...and still craving for more.

I also love reading a lot of books, and traveling to learn about different places and cultures, constantly.
